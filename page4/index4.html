Our Service

The full list of our bureau's services

Full‑Cycle General Contracting & Turnkey Construction

We take full responsibility for your project from the very first site inspection to the final handover. Our team manages all subcontractors, procurement, scheduling, and quality control. You receive a move‑in ready building with a single point of contact, fixed deadlines, and transparent cost reporting.

Architectural Design, BIM Modeling & 3D Visualization

We translate your ideas into precise construction documents using advanced BIM (Building Information Modeling) software. Our architects produce detailed floor plans, sections, and facades, while 3D visualisation gives you a photorealistic walkthrough of every interior and exterior space before a single brick is laid.

Comprehensive Renovation & Premium Interior Finishing

This service covers the complete transformation of existing spaces. We handle demolition, wall erection, screeding, floor covering, tiling, decorative painting, and custom joinery. Using only high‑end materials, we ensure flawless execution with careful attention to acoustic and thermal insulation.

Engineering Systems Installation (MEP)

We design and install all mechanical, electrical, and plumbing systems required for modern buildings. This includes HVAC (heating, ventilation, air conditioning), low‑current networks (data, security), lighting, switchgear, water supply, drainage, and fire protection. All work complies with strict safety and energy‑efficiency standards.

Exterior Cladding, Roofing & Structural Reinforcement

Our team restores and protects the building envelope. We offer ventilated facades, natural stone cladding, composite panels, and all types of roofing (flat, pitched, metal, shingle). In addition, we reinforce concrete columns, beams, and steel frameworks to extend the structure’s lifespan and meet updated seismic or load requirements.

Site Preparation, Utility Connections & Landscaping

We prepare your land for construction by clearing vegetation, grading the terrain, and excavating foundations. We also arrange official connections to municipal water, sewage, gas, and power grids. Finally, our landscaping team installs paving, retaining walls, irrigation systems, and planting to create a finished outdoor environment.
