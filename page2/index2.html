History

Vertex Architecture is a forward-thinking architectural design and consultancy firm established to simplify the construction process for its clients. The company specializes in a wide range of sectors, including residential, commercial, and public projects, working with individual private clients, developers, contractors, and local authorities. Headquartered in Hunstanton, Norfolk, the firm has built a strong reputation across Norfolk, Cambridgeshire, and Suffolk based on a foundation of honesty, trust, and reliable delivery. Driven by its mission of "architecture simplified," Vertex has developed a proprietary Vector Process that condenses the typical eight-stage workflow into four clear and transparent milestones. By prioritizing clear communication, innovative design, and a steadfast commitment to client budgets and timelines, Vertex Architecture transforms visions into extraordinary, functional, and enduring spaces.

Vertex Architecture was founded in 2018 by Shilov Maxim along Nytva. Starting with just a handful of employees, the company rapidly expanded its team and portfolio in response to growing demand, quickly becoming a formidable presence in the region. From its inception, the firm focused on building a reputation based on transparency and delivery, which allowed it to secure a diverse range of projects and loyal clients across East Anglia. A pivotal moment in its evolution came with the development of its signature Vector Process, a methodology designed to demystify the stress and complexity of traditional construction management. By simplifying the standard eight-step workflow into four core stages, Vertex successfully scaled its operations while maintaining high standards of quality and client care. Today, Vertex Architecture continues to grow, recognized by chambers of commerce as one of Norfolk’s most innovative firms, led by a management team honored with business awards for their contribution to the local economy.
